# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: November 1, 2025**

## Overview

Pet Health Log ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Email address (for authentication)
- Display name (optional)

### Pet Information
- Pet names, species, breed, date of birth
- Health records, vet visit details
- Photos you upload
- Reminder preferences

### Usage Data
- App usage analytics
- Device information
- Error logs

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the service
- To send reminders and notifications
- To improve app functionality
- To provide customer support
- To process subscription payments

## Data Storage

- All data is encrypted at rest
- Stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- Files stored in secure cloud storage
- AI processing uses OpenAI API (data not retained by OpenAI)

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. We share data only with:
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication
- **OpenAI**: AI summarization (not stored by OpenAI)
- **RevenueCat**: Subscription management
- **Firebase**: Push notifications

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Export your data (CSV/PDF)
- Delete your account and all data
- Opt-out of analytics

## Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely
- Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days

## Children's Privacy

Pet Health Log is not intended for users under 13 years old.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes via email.

## Contact Us

For privacy concerns, email: privacy@pethealthlog.com

## GDPR Compliance

If you are in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to be forgotten
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
